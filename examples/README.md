## Invention / 发明

The synthetic concept is a rail-guidance arrangement with a compliant guide and a replaceable contact insert. The draft keeps the arrangement and intended verification separate from results. See [the bilingual worked example](synthetic-invention.md).

该合成概念是一种导轨导向结构，包括顺应性导向件和可更换接触嵌件；草案将结构方案、拟验证事项与试验结果严格区分。见[双语工作示例](synthetic-invention.md)。

## Utility model / 实用新型

The synthetic concept is a product housing, guide groove, retaining shoulder, and removable cover. The independent claim is directed to product structure and relationships, not a method. See [the bilingual worked example](synthetic-utility-model.md).

该合成概念包括产品壳体、导向槽、保持肩和可拆盖；独立权利要求以产品结构及其关系为主题，而非方法。见[双语工作示例](synthetic-utility-model.md)。

The examples are workflow demonstrations, not novelty opinions or filing-ready claims.
